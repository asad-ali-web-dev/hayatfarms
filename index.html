Know Us

Hayat Farms deals in crop and livestock farming businesses, promoting the mechanisation, Climate Smart Agricultural Practices and Innovative techniques among the local small-to-large farming communities, merging the indigenous and advanced knowledge and information for the sustainable future of the earth.

Hayat Farms is a farm comprised of two sections Livestock & Agriculture situated in the most fertile plains of Pakistan in the suburbs of Pakpattan District.

Mr. Aamer Hayat Bhandara is currently managing the farm, is a commercial livestock farmer, and grows wheat, maize, rice, sorghum, potatoes and cotton. Mr. Bhandara is also a politician in Pakpattan, south Punjab, and is an elected member of the district council since 2019 and on various district -division- and- provincial level committees on agriculture and price control. He is also a fellow with LEAD (Leadership for Environment and Development), an independent policy think tank focused on policy research, public policy engagement, and leadership development in the public, private and not-for-profit sectors. Educated in Pakistan and Australia, Mr. Bhandara has served as a guest lecturer at different universities in Pakistan. He is an outspoken leader for small farmers, advocating for improved support for small farmers and raising awareness of the challenges via the media. He has worked to reduce the role of middlemen in the dairy and horticulture sectors and wants to attract more young people to careers in agriculture.
